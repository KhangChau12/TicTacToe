ABOUT THE AI
?The Minimax algorithm examines all possible moves and chooses the optimal one. Unlike Q-learning, Minimax works perfectly without training.

This AI uses the Minimax algorithm with Alpha-Beta pruning, an optimal method for 3x3 tic-tac-toe.

At the highest difficulty, the AI cannot be defeated - it will always win or draw.

The Minimax algorithm predicts all possible moves and selects the optimal one, assuming the opponent also plays optimally.
